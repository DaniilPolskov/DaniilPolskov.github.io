UNIHERTZ TANK REVIEW: 22,000 MAH

At the end of last year, Unihertz introduced a new protected smartphone with the most capacious battery among similar devices – Tank. The gadget got a 22,000 mAh battery! Yes, I was not mistaken in the zeros. That's how much. In fact, it is a portable Power Bank with a built-in smartphone. Looking ahead, I must say that I had to check the autonomy of this device for two weeks. The fact is that with normal operation, the Tank can work for 6-8 days. And the video plays without stopping at maximum brightness for almost 50 hours!
